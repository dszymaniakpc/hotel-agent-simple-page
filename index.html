About Our Resort

Discover the Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian experience

Located on the pristine shores of the Red Sea, Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian Resort offers a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and authentic Nubian culture.

Our resort features 395 elegantly appointed rooms and suites, each designed to provide maximum comfort and relaxation. Guests can enjoy our private beach, multiple swimming pools, and a range of dining options serving international and local cuisines.

The resort is ideally situated just 30 minutes from Marsa Alam International Airport and offers easy access to the region's famous diving spots, including Elphinstone Reef and Sataya Dolphin Reef.

Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or an adventure-filled holiday, our dedicated staff is committed to ensuring your stay is nothing short of exceptional.
